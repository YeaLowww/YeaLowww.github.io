RPG Assassins Creed reference

Developing a game on Unreal Engine 5 using Blueprints inspired by the Assassin's Creed series was extremely exciting. This was a new experience for me, as it was my first time working with visual programming, and I felt that every stage of development was like solving a complex puzzle.

This game combines stealth mechanics, parkour, and a combat system, as well as RPG elements such as character development and decision-making that affect the story and a lot of other features that needed to be implemented

This project greatly expanded my understanding of the possibilities of game engines and game design. During my work, I tried to look at the game from the developers' point of view, analyzing what elements might be interesting or useful. I also looked for creative solutions for mechanics and gameplay, trying to implement interesting ideas using Blueprints.

This experience inspired me to further study game engineering and opened new horizons in understanding programming.
